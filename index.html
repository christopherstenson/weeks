How many weeks/days do you have left?

On average in the US, a person can expect to live 79 years. Women typically live for 82 years. Men typically live for 77.

Input your birthday and this site will show you how many weeks/days you have left to live, on average. You may wish to adjust the age of your death to experiment with different indexes.
